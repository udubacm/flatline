Teaching Award Recipients

making your cse experience even better

The UW CSE ACM Teaching Award is given each year to a faculty member elected by the undergraduate majors in the department. The ACM Chapter is proud to organize this process and to recognize some of our outstanding teachers. Previous winners are listed below as well as on a plaque displayed in the department. Each year's winner is also recognized at the department graduation ceremony.
